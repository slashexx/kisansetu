About Us

Welcome to KisanSetu, a government initiative dedicated to strengthening India’s agricultural sector by providing farmers with direct access to markets. Our platform aims to empower farmers across the nation by ensuring fair and transparent trade, removing intermediaries, and creating an ecosystem of trust and efficiency in the agricultural supply chain.

Our Mission

At KisanSetu, our mission is to facilitate sustainable development in the agricultural sector by offering farmers a reliable and accessible platform to sell their produce directly to buyers. We are committed to promoting rural prosperity by enhancing market access and ensuring equitable pricing for farmers, while also delivering high-quality produce to consumers.

How It Works

Through KisanSetu, farmers can register and list their produce on the platform, ensuring visibility to a wide range of buyers, including wholesalers, retailers, and institutions. Buyers, in turn, benefit from direct access to fresh, high-quality produce at competitive prices. The platform is designed to support farmers by providing:

      Assured Market Access: Farmers have access to a nationwide network of buyers, reducing their dependency on traditional markets.
      Fair Pricing Mechanism: Farmers are assured of fair and transparent pricing, supported by government guidelines and market trends.
      Simplified Logistics: By connecting directly with buyers, farmers can streamline their sales, ensuring efficient and timely transactions.

Government Commitment to Farmers

KisanSetu reflects the Government of India’s commitment to improving the livelihoods of farmers and ensuring their participation in the national economy. By leveraging technology and digital platforms, the government seeks to create a sustainable agricultural ecosystem that benefits both farmers and consumers. This initiative aligns with the broader vision of achieving food security, rural development, and economic empowerment in the agricultural sector.
